---
title: "What is OpenBat"
description: "AI chatbot analytics platform that turns every conversation into actionable insight"
---

OpenBat is an AI chatbot analytics platform — think of it as Google Analytics for AI chatbots. It ingests your chatbot conversations via SDK or API, runs AI-powered analysis on every message, and surfaces actionable insights about user sentiment, assistant performance, and revenue signals.

Whether you're a customer success team investigating frustration patterns, a product manager tracking feature gaps, or a developer monitoring chatbot quality, OpenBat gives you the data to understand not just *what* users are asking — but *how well* your chatbot is performing.

## Key capabilities

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Sentiment analysis" icon="face-smile" href="/features/analytics-dashboard">
    Score every message for sentiment and track trends over time. Identify your most dissatisfied users and investigate their conversations.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Behavior flagging" icon="flag" href="/features/analysis-config">
    Detect signals like churn risk, buying intent, competitor mentions, and confused users. Flag assistant behaviors like hallucination, dodging, and over-apologizing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Semantic search" icon="magnifying-glass" href="/features/deep-search">
    Search conversations by meaning, not keywords. Find patterns like "users asking about pricing" or "billing issues" across your entire conversation history.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Workflow automation" icon="bolt" href="/features/workflows">
    Trigger alerts and actions when specific conversation patterns are detected. Send notifications to Slack, Discord, or custom webhooks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Client tracking" icon="building" href="/features/client-management">
    Track organizations and individual users who interact with your chatbot, with revenue metrics (MRR, plan tier) and sentiment scores.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SDK integration" icon="code" href="/integrations/sdk-reference">
    Connect with the Vercel AI SDK, TypeScript, Python, or REST API. Start analyzing conversations in under 5 minutes.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who is OpenBat for

- **Customer success teams** investigating frustration patterns and identifying at-risk accounts
- **Product managers** tracking feature gaps, capability issues, and user intent trends
- **Chatbot developers** monitoring assistant quality, hallucination rates, and resolution outcomes
- **Support leads** setting up automated alerts for negative sentiment and escalation triggers
