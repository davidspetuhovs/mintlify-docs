Every OpenBat account belongs to an organization. Organizations group your chatbots, conversations, and team members under a single workspace. You can invite teammates, assign roles, and control who can manage resources.

## Organizations

When you sign up for the first time, OpenBat automatically creates an organization named after your email prefix. There is no manual setup step.

If you belong to multiple organizations, use the organization switcher in the sidebar to move between them. The switcher only appears when you have more than one organization.

## Roles

Each member of an organization has one of three roles:

| Role | Capabilities |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access. Rename and delete the organization. Manage all members and chatbots. May be able to transfer ownership. |
| **Admin** | Invite and remove members. Create and manage chatbots. Cannot delete the organization or transfer ownership. |
| **Member** | View access to assigned chatbots. Typically cannot invite members or create chatbots. |

## Inviting members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the members page">
    Navigate to **Members** in the sidebar, or go directly to [app.openbat.dev/platform/members](https://app.openbat.dev/platform/members).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click invite member">
    Click the **Invite member** button. This is visible to owners and admins only.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter details">
    Enter the email address of the person you want to invite and select their role (admin or member).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Recipient joins">
    The invited user receives an email with a link to create an account (or sign in if they already have one). Once they accept, they appear in the members list with their assigned role.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Removing members

Owners and admins can remove any member from the members page. Click the remove action next to the member you want to remove. Access is revoked immediately — the removed member can no longer see the organization or any of its chatbots.

## Per-chatbot access control

In addition to organization-level roles, you can restrict which members have access to a specific chatbot. Open the chatbot's **Settings** page and navigate to the **Members** tab to manage per-chatbot access. For more details on chatbot settings, see [Chatbot settings](/guides/chatbot-settings).

## Organization settings

Owners can rename or delete the organization from the organization settings page at [app.openbat.dev/platform/settings](https://app.openbat.dev/platform/settings).

![Organization settings](/images/org-settings.png)

### Rename

Change the display name of your organization. The name appears in the sidebar and in team invitations. Only owners can rename the organization.

### Delete

<Warning>
  Deleting an organization is permanent and cannot be undone. All chatbots, conversations, and analysis data belonging to the organization are permanently removed.
</Warning>

Only owners can delete an organization. A confirmation step prevents accidental deletion.
