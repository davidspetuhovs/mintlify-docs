title: "Team management"

---

OpenBat uses organizations to group teams and chatbots. Invite colleagues, assign roles, and manage your organization settings from the platform sidebar.

## Organization members

![Organization members](/images/org-members.png)

The members page shows everyone in your organization with their email, role, and join date.

### Inviting members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the members page">
    Click **Members** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the invitation">
    Click **+ Invite member**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter email">
    Enter the new member's email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign a role">
    Select a role for the member.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invitation">
    Submit the invitation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The member receives an email invitation. Once accepted, they appear in the members table and can access all chatbots in the organization.

### Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access. Can manage members, settings, billing, and delete the organization. |
| **Admin** | Can manage chatbots, view analytics, and configure settings. Cannot delete the organization. |
| **Member** | Can view dashboards, browse conversations, and use search. Cannot modify settings. |

## Organization settings

![Organization settings](/images/org-settings.png)

Navigate to **Settings** in the sidebar to manage organization-level configuration.

- **Organization name** — Change the display name that appears across the platform. Enter a new name and click save.

- **Danger zone** — Permanently delete the organization.

<Warning>
  Deleting your organization permanently removes all chatbots, conversations, messages, and analytics data for every chatbot in the organization. This cannot be undone.
</Warning>

<Info>
  Organization-level members have access to all chatbots. To manage access to individual chatbots, use the Members tab in [chatbot settings](/guides/settings).
</Info>
