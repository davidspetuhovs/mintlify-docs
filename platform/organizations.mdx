---
title: "Organizations"
description: "Create, switch, rename, and delete organizations that group your chatbots and team members"
---

Organizations are the top-level container in OpenBat. Each organization has its own set of chatbots, members, and settings. You can belong to multiple organizations and switch between them.

## Switch organizations

Click the **organization switcher** at the top of the sidebar to switch between organizations or create a new one.

<Frame>
  <img src="/docs/state-screenshots/state-platform-org-switcher.png" alt="Organization switcher dropdown showing current org and Create organization option" />
</Frame>

The switcher displays your current organization and lists all organizations you belong to. Select a different org to switch context — the dashboard updates to show that organization's chatbots.

## Create an organization

Click **+ Create organization** in the organization switcher dropdown. Enter a name and confirm. You're switched to the new organization immediately.

## Rename an organization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to settings">
    Go to **Settings** in the sidebar (at `/platform/settings`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit the name">
    Change the **Organization name** field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. The name updates across the platform, including the sidebar switcher.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Delete an organization

<Warning>
  Deleting an organization permanently removes all its data — chatbots, conversations, analysis results, workflows, and member associations. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to settings">
    Go to **Settings** at `/platform/settings`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scroll to the danger zone">
    Find the **Danger zone** section at the bottom of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Delete organization">
    Click the **Delete organization** button and confirm in the dialog.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Only organization **Owners** can rename or delete an organization.
